About Us

Rev. Jo Harrison is a passionate leader and minister who has dedicated her life to serving God and empowering women. She is the branch pastor of Christian Restoration Ministries International and the President of the Jo Harrison Ministries, which runs the Ladies Arise program in London and Ghana

Ladies Arise

The Ladies Arise program is a dynamic ministry program for women led by Rev. Jo Harrison. The program is designed to empower women with the tools they need to thrive in every area of their lives. Through fellowship, prayer, and teaching, women are able to discover their purpose and become all that God has called them to be. Join us for our next event and experience the transformative power of the Ladies Arise program.

Rev. Jo Harrison accepted the call to rally women from all walks of life with what started out as a desire, birthed in her heart. Years on and it has now developed into a potent mega platform which God uses to transform lives, mend the broken whilst bringing healing, peace and joy in the lives of many. Indeed when God calls you, He also equips and makes provision for you so that all glory will go to His name

Each year, there is a steady increase in women attending the Ladies Arise Conference seeking for an increased revelation of God's Word and an intimate time to experience the power of God, sisterhood, fellowship and networking

Christian Restoration Ministries International (CRMI), a ministry that was birthed in 1998 by Apostle Dr. Emmanuel Eyim-Danquah has the mandate to glorify God by leading people to know Jesus and the benefit of his family, equiping them as true disciples to serve both in the church and in the world.
One of the programs that Jo Harrison Ministries runs is Ladies Arise, a program that has empowered women in London and Ghana to rise up and fulfill their God-given destiny. What We Do

We are committed to serving our community and spreading the word of God in everything we do

Preaching

As a pastor, Rev.Jo Harrison is passionate about preaching the word of God and spreading the gospel to all those who will hear it. Through her preaching, Rev.Jo aims to inspire and encourage individuals to develop a deeper relationship with Christ and to find hope and strength in His message. She believes that by sharing God's love and wisdom, we can make a positive impact on the world and help to create a more compassionate and just society

Conseling

One of her key goals as a lady pastor is to see people triumph through the hurdles of life by offering counseling, or nurturing leadership qualities. Her aim is to help all people to draw closer to God, triumph over life's hurdles, and help you realize your fullest potential in Christ.

Parnership with other ministries

We believe that we can achieve more when we work together with other ministries and organizations.Through these partnerships, we are able to leverage our collective resources and expertise to make a greater impact in our community and beyond. Whether through joint events, collaborative programs, or mutual support, we are committed to building strong and lasting partnerships with other ministries.
